About DAMS

DAMS is the first self-financed institute in Kanpur to impart management education in the form of MBA, BBA, BCA.

We change lives by helping our students to develop the confidence, knowledge, skills and adaptability. They need for a successful career and fulfilling life objective. We produce world class research that helps us understand the world and change it for the better.
