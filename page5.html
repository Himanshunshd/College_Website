Events

"I believe that education is all about being excited about something. Seeing passion and enthusiasm helps push an educational message."

Energy - The Athletic Meet

VSEF organizes a two-day athletic meet called Energy, providing students an opportunity to relax, rejuvenate, and explore sports and athletics. The event features various track and field competitions with enthusiastic participation from multiple teams.

Srijan

An innovation to creativity, Srijan showcases talent, dance, drama, singing, and innovative ideas. The event highlights student enthusiasm and creativity while delivering a memorable experience to spectators.

Swarup's Mega Job Fair

Conducted every two years, the Mega Job Fair connects students with leading companies and employment opportunities. It serves as a bridge between recruiters and aspiring professionals.

Seminars

Seminars conducted by renowned speakers and experts provide students with insights into current trends, industry developments, and emerging global issues.

MBA & BBA Industrial Visit

Students visit leading organizations such as Bajaj Auto, Britannia, Tata Motors, Havells, and others to gain practical exposure and interact with industry leaders.
